Sidechain compression and the pump

A kick drum feeds the sidechain (the key input) of a compressor on a sustained pad. Every kick ducks the pad and it springs back over the release, the breathing pump of house, techno, and modern pop. Press Start, then Play, and turn the sidechain off and on to hear what it does.
