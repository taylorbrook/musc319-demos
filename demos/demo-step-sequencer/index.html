Step sequencer: program a beat, then give it a groove

Click the grid to place kick, snare, and hi-hat hits on the sixteen steps of one bar. Click a lit cell again to turn it into an accent (a louder hit, the way a high velocity reads). Set the tempo, push swing to delay the off-beat sixteenths so the pattern grooves instead of marching, and raise humanize to loosen the machine-tight quantize. Press Start to enable sound, then Play.
