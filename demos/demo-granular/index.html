Granular synthesis: turn a short sound into a cloud of grains

The synth reads a short source sound and sprays it out as overlapping grains, each a few to tens of milliseconds long. Raise the grain density to thicken a single fragment into a continuous cloud, add pitch and position spray to make it shimmer, and freeze the read position to sustain one moment as a held texture. Press Start to enable sound, then Play.
