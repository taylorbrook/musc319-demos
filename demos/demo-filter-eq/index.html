EQ & filters: shape the spectrum

A harmonically rich tone runs through one filter. Pick its shape (lowpass, highpass, bandpass, peaking), set the cutoff and Q, and watch the live spectrum redistribute its energy as you hear it change. The same control used correctively cuts a problem; swept, it becomes a compositional gesture. Press Start, then Play.

The spectrum (live FFT)

The curve is the live energy across the spectrum. A lowpass rolls off the highs, a highpass the lows, a bandpass keeps a band, and peaking boosts or cuts around the cutoff. Raise Q to hear the filter ring at its corner.
