Spatial I: place a sound on the 8-channel array

One source is sent to eight loudspeakers arranged in a ring, the format of the WinterBang concert. The source is panned with amplitude panning (VBAP): only the two speakers nearest its direction are driven, with the loudness held constant as it moves. Set its position with the slider or by dragging on the array, then press Diffuse to send it on a performed trajectory. Use stereo speakers or headphones. Press Start, then Play.

Step 1: enable sound

Audio is off.

Step 2: place and move the source

Watch the gains as the source moves: only the nearest pair is ever driven, and they crossfade so the total power stays at 1, which is why a panned source does not get louder or softer as it travels. The stereo fold for your laptop collapses front and back onto one line; in the real ring those are different speakers, which is what the concert format gives you.
