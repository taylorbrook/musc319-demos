Spectrum analyzer: see the signal as spectrum

Before any processing, make the signal legible by seeing it. Drive a source and read its live spectrum (FFT): a sine is one peak, a sawtooth is a stack of harmonics, noise fills the whole band. This is the view every later EQ, compressor, and meter reads. Press Start, then Play.
