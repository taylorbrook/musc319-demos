ADSR envelope: shape a note over time

A sampler (and every synth voice) shapes a note's level over time with an ADSR envelope: attack in, decay down to a sustain level held while you hold the note, then release out when you let go. Hold the Play-note button and watch the envelope draw itself. Press Start, then play a note.

Live output (time domain)

Attack and release set the in/out times; decay falls to the sustain level, which holds while you hold the note. Short attack + short release is percussive; long attack + long release is a slow swell.
