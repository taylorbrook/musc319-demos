Club rhythms: four-on-the-floor against the breakbeat

Pick a genre and the grid loads its signature rhythm at its own tempo and feel. Listen for one thing: house and techno put a kick on every beat (four-on-the-floor), while jungle and UK garage skip that steady pulse and run a syncopated breakbeat instead. Switch genres and follow where the kick lands. You can edit any cell, change the tempo, or push the swing to hear the garage shuffle. Press Start to enable sound, then Play.
