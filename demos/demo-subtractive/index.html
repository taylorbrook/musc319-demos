Subtractive synthesis: start rich, then remove

The classic synth signal path: a harmonically rich oscillator → a filter that removes or emphasizes part of it → an amplifier shaped by an envelope. Pick a waveshape, set the cutoff and resonance, and play notes to hear the filter envelope sweep and the amp ADSR shape the level. Press Start, then play a note.
