Automation as composition: shape a section without new notes

The loop keeps playing the same notes. You draw an automation curve across the bar by dragging in the lane, pick which parameter it drives, and the sound follows your curve every loop. A rising cutoff opens the section up; a swell breathes; a pulse makes the filter throb. This is form built from how a parameter moves, not from which notes play. Press Start, then Play, then draw a curve or load a shape.
