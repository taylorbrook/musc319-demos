Additive synthesis: build a waveshape from harmonics

Set the amplitude of the first eight harmonics with the sliders. The synth sums those sine partials into one tone, and the single-cycle waveshape they build is drawn on the left as the harmonic spectrum on the right changes. Press Start to enable sound, then Play.
